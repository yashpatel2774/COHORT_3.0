SHINSEI VILLAGE

Genuine Storytelling Project

VISION

Shinsei Village is a collection featuring 1,200 demigod monkeys, unraveling a mysterious case to save their village.

The project combines storytelling, immersive art and community-driven adventures into a unique web3 experience.

Each stage of development delivers rewards and interactive experiences for holders through evolving story chapters.

Weekly puzzles and community decisions determine the fate of the village and unlock future mysteries.
